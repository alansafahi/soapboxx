For the **SoapBox Super App**, an **Image Gallery** page can serve as a powerful visual space for community connection, spiritual inspiration, and storytelling. Here's a detailed breakdown of **what to include** and **how to design it** for optimal **UI/UX**, especially within a **faith-based context**.

---

## 🖼️ IMAGE GALLERY: Purpose

To visually inspire, connect, and engage users through faith-oriented content—encouraging reflection, community, worship, and personal growth.

---

## ✅ CONTENT CATEGORIES (What to Include)

Organize images into **tagged sections or carousels** for better discovery:

### 🔹 1. **Moments of Worship**

* Church services
* Baptisms
* Altar calls
* Candlelight vigils

### 🔹 2. **User-Generated Testimonies**

* Before/after photos
* Visual testimonials with scripture overlays
* Transformation stories

### 🔹 3. **Events & Gatherings**

* Retreats
* Youth camps
* Conferences
* Community outreach (feeding homeless, mission trips, etc.)

### 🔹 4. **Sacred Spaces**

* Beautiful church architecture
* Prayer rooms
* Nature-as-sanctuary (sunrises, oceans, forests, etc.)

### 🔹 5. **Inspirational Verses (Visual Verse Cards)**

* Verse graphics formatted for sharing
* Scripture + artwork combo
* Option for users to "favorite" or “save to devotionals”

### 🔹 6. **Art & Creativity**

* Christian artwork (paintings, calligraphy, cross-stitch, etc.)
* Children’s Bible art
* Visual poetry or faith-based typographic design

### 🔹 7. **Daily Inspiration**

* A “Photo of the Day” or “God’s Moment Today” section
* Auto-curated or admin-selected daily photos

---

## 🧭 NAVIGATION & FILTERS

* 🔍 **Search Bar** — search by tag, verse, date, uploader
* 📌 **Filter by Category** — worship, scripture, events, etc.
* ⏱️ **Sort Options** — most recent, most liked, staff picks
* 🙋‍♂️ **My Uploads / Favorites** tab

---

## 🧑‍🎨 UI/UX DESIGN PRINCIPLES

### 🌟 1. **Minimalist & Sacred Aesthetic**

* White or soft neutral backgrounds with subtle gold or deep indigo accents
* Rounded corners, soft drop shadows
* Focus on peace, space, and spiritual tone

### 📱 2. **Mobile-First Design**

* Grid-based masonry layout (Pinterest-style)
* Infinite scroll or “Load More” button
* Tap-to-expand with modal view (caption, verse, user info)

### ❤️ 3. **Interaction Features**

* ❤️ Like
* 💬 Comment
* 📤 Share (Instagram, WhatsApp, inside SoapBox)
* ✨ Save to Devotional Routine or Daily Journal

### 🧵 4. **Story Mode (Optional)**

* Add Stories or Slideshow-style reels: “This Week at My Church,” “My Faith Journey,” “Verse Reflections”

---

## 🔒 MODERATION & PRIVACY

* Allow users to choose visibility: **Public / Church Only / Private**
* All uploads go through **moderation queue** (optional AI + human)
* Church Admins can manage member-submitted media

---

## 🧩 FUTURE INTEGRATIONS

* **Live Gallery from Events** — syncs with church livestream/photos in real-time
* **Image-to-Devotional Tool** — select an image and automatically build a reflection using AI and Scripture

---

## 🎯 GOALS THIS PAGE SHOULD SUPPORT

1. Strengthen community identity
2. Visually reinforce key themes: love, sacrifice, grace, faith
3. Encourage users to share and participate
4. Provide inspiration in bite-sized, visual format
